TOUR DETAILS

DAY 1 - ARRIVE KIRUNA
14-JAN - Arrive Kiruna Airport Meet greet and transfer to hotel. Check in to your hotel by 1400 hrs. Early check-in is subject to availability). Buffet Dinner at the hotel (Local cuisine)
CHASING NORTHERN LIGHTS WITH HUSKIES
On this Northern Light Husky Ride we chase the Northern Light – Aurora Borealis – with the dog sled. Be a passenger or steer a dog sled at night time. Whether you decide to be a passenger on the guides dog sled or want to share a sled with another guest, it is always special to be out with the dogs at night. A sky covered with glittering stars or a full moon in the wilderness is one of the most amazing sights. Howling huskies by the campfire and the Northern lights dancing above us can make it a magical night.
Overnight stay in KIRUNA

DAY 2 - KIRUNA 15-JAN - Breakfast
Visit the World famous LKAB IRON MINE TOUR. The mine in Kiruna is the world’s largest underground iron-ore mine. LKAB is currently mining down to level 1365 metres. Our guide will show you around the visitor premises that cover 20,000 square metres. This is an authentic mining environment where displays, films and machines illustrate ore mining and the production of iron.
SNOW MOBILE TOUR
Meet your guide directly at your Kiruna hotel, and head out of town. Escape into nature, and even learn how to drive your own snowmobile, on this intrepid journey into Sweden’s Arctic wilderness. After a training session and test drive, you’ll begin your multi-hour adventure. Admire the landscapes, glimpse local wildlife and, if you’re lucky, even see the Northern Lights. Buffet Dinner at the hotel (Local cuisine)
Overnight stay in KIRUNA
DAY 3 - KIRUNA - 16-JAN- Breakfast - Enjoy TOUR OF ICE HOTEL
Enjoy a guided tour of ICEHOTEL, the innovative hotel made of ice and snow, which is also one of Sweden’s largest and most unique private art exhibitions. The guided tour takes 30-45 minutes where the guide tells about the work, history and creation.
VISIT TO SAMI VILLAGE
Take a day trip to the Sámi village and experience a glimpse of the traditional lifestyle of the Sámi people. Get up close and personal with Laplands most iconic creature – the reindeer. During this exciting adventure you will have an opportunity to feed the reindeer and to absorb the magical relationship between the Sámi and their most cherished resource. Buffet Dinner at the hotel (Local cuisine) Overnight stay in KIRUNA
DAY 4- KIRUNA - STOCKHOLM - 17-JAN - Breakfast
Check out of the hotel and transfer to Kiruna Airport Fly Kiruna to Stockholm, Arrive Stockholm and transfer to hotel, Explore Stockholm from the vantage point of its waterways on this canal tour. Cruise through the central islands of the city's archipelago, relaxing while you chug past the neighborhoods of Södermalm and Slussen, then out to the leafy tranquil island of Djurgården, which is home to the Prins Eugens Waldemarsudde Museum and the Vasa Museum.
Buffet Dinner at the hotel (Local cuisine) Overnight stay in STOCKHOLM

STOCKHOLM GUIDED CITY TOUR
Get an in-depth perspective on Stockholm on this guided walking tour. Visit Gamla Stan (the Old Town), view point, Royal Palace change of guard. Along the way, gain insight into both the Swedish capital's history and its modern- day culture from your guide.
